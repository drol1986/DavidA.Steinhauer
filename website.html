        Professor Steinhauer has been with TCC for over 30 years. During this time he has done extensive work with industry designing programs of study and teaching classes in several areas to meet the needs of local industry. These include Drafting & Design, Computer-Aided Drafting, Rapid Prototyping, Occupational Safety including OSHA Regulations, Materials and Processes of Industry and Blueprint Reading. He is a Certified Autodesk Professional in both AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor software’s.
